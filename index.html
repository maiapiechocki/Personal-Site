Hello! My name is Maia Piechocki, and I am a current undergraduate at USC pursuing a BS in computer science with an emphasis in embedded systems. My passion is directed towards the intersection of hardware and software, ranging from applications in microelectronics to developing probabilistic models of machine learning.

The field of cs is broad, but i'm willing to work hard, experiment, and put in the effort to learn and develop as a person and as an academic. The field of CS is broad, and I'm driven by a deep epistemic curiosity—a willingness to work hard, experiment, and put in the effort to learn not just the what, but the how and why behind a concept. The most valuable asset to have in this world is experience, as we are the amalgamation of what we've learned and what we do with that knowledge. Outside of academics, I enjoy making music (currently experimenting with Tidal), hiking, ametuer rocketry, and baking for friends.

Ultimately, I want to accrue experience, equip myself for the real world, and learn from a self-driven curiosity about such systems that govern our society through hard work and adaptability. The only source of Knowledge is experience; I have no special talent. I am only passionately curious
