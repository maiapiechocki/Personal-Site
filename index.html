Hello! My name is Maia Piechocki, and I am a current undergraduate at USC pursuing a BS in computer science with an emphasis in embedded systems. My passion is directed towards the intersection of hardware and software, ranging from applications in microelectronics to developing probabilistic models in machine learning.

The field of cs is broad, but i'm driven by a vehement curiousity, willingness to work hard, experiment, and put in the effort to learn and develop as a person and as an academic. The most valuable asset one can possess is experience, as we are the amalgamation of what we've learned and what we do with that knowledge. Outside of academics, I enjoy making music (specifically with Tidal), hiking, ametuer rocketry, and baking for friends.

Ultimately, I want to accrue experience, equip myself with tools to solve today's computing challenges, and learn from a self-driven curiosity about such systems that govern our society through diligence and adaptability.
